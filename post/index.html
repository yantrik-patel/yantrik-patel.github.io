Project 7: ML_Python Web Scraping and Sentiment Analysis

In this project, I have done Webscraping of Food Reviews of one of the pizza restaurants on Yelp.com and carried out sentiment analysis. In Python, I have used “BeautifulSoup” for webscraping and NLP(Natural Language Processing) ML models like VADER, ROBERTA and BERT for sentiment analysis. My final discovery is ROBERTA is more confident while classifying reviews compared to VADER. The BERT is very robust and state of the art model which classify the sentiment on a scale of 1 to 5.
