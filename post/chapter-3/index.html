Project 3: SQL_UK Accident 2016 Analysis

November 1, 2022

In this project, I have tried to analyze the accidents reported in the year 2016 in UK. The dataset is publicly available on Kaggle from the year 1979 till 2020. I have cleaned the data using Excel and used MySQL workbench to query the data and get the insights. The dataset have many information like dates, districts, highway authority, road condtion, light and weather conditions, area type etc. The size of data is 136000 records and more than 12 attributes or columns. It’s an interesting dataset revealing the stats about place and conditions under which accidents took place.
